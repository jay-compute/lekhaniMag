Pranaya S. Ayyala


Pranaya S. Ayyala is the editor and founder of Lekhani. She was raised in both the United States and India, indulging and identifying with both cultures. Currently, she is pursuing her degree in accounting and minors in creative writing, and has worked as an editorial intern at Lantern Review in the past year. In her free time, she enjoys video games and books - both always accompanied with a warm cup of coffee.
