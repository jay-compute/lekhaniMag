About Us Page

Pranaya S. Ayyala

Co-editor and Founder

Pranaya S. Ayyala is the co-editor and founder of Lekhani. She is Telugu, from the South Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. Currently, she is working towards a degree in Accounting and Creative Writing, and spends her free time either gaming or reading. Her writing work is featured on Lantern Review.

Jevaria Bashir

Reader: Visual Arts

Jevaria Bashir is the reader for visual art for Lekahni. She was born in New York and is ethnically Pakistani, specifically from Azad Kashmir. Jevaria is currently completing a Computer Science major alongside a certificate for Quantum Computing. Outside of academic pursuits, she enjoys drawing, preferably digital painting, and has been doing so for 13 years.

Angel Mary Joseph

Outreach/Social Media and Reader: Visual Media

Angel Mary Joseph is part of the outreach team at Lekhani and a visual media reader for the journal. They are a Malayali international student from Kerala, India currently doing a Psychology and Film and Media studies (Critical Studies track) double major program with a concentration in Business at the University of Pittsburgh. They are currently involved in research on minority representation in the popular Indian film culture of Bollywood and working at the university as the Lead EcoRep, educating peers on sustainability. Outside academics, they enjoy reading, dancing, and creative photography.

Ruchi Hegde

Web Developer

Ruchi Hegde is the web developer for Lekhani. She works on coding the website, app, and maintaining and improving the user interfaces. Originally from Philadelphia, but ethnically Kannadiga, Ruchi is a second year computer science and business major with a minor in Mandarin Chinese at the University of Pittsburgh. Along with Lekhani, Ruchi is involved in club swimming, Dhirana, and other computer science clubs. Outside of class, She enjoys working out, painting, and reading.
