Anvi Joshi


Anvi Joshi is the reader for poetry at Lekhani. Born in New Jersey, she is ethnically Marathi, from Maharashtra, India. She is currently pursuing a degree in Psychology with minors in Linguistics and Criminal Justice. She enjoys embroidery and yoga in her free time. She has previously won honorable mentions in poetry at the regional Scholastic Arts and Writing Contest, and for Feature Writing at the G.S.S.P.A conference.
