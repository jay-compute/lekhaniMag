Angel Mary Joseph


Angel Mary Joseph is part of the outreach team at Lekhani and a visual media reader for the journal. They are a Malayali international student from Kerala, India currently doing Psychology and Film and Media studies with a concentration in Business Studies. They are currently working on minority representation in Bollywood. They are a performance artist and visual arts photographer working under the name Apsara On Film.
